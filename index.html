Everything You Need

Cycles Render Engine

Cycles is Blender's robust, unbiased path-tracing engine, renowned for producing breathtakingly realistic renders. It boasts real-time viewport preview, versatile CPU and GPU rendering, PBR shaders, HDR lighting, and comprehensive VR rendering support.

Masterful Modeling

Blender provides a comprehensive suite of modeling tools that streamline the creation, transformation, and refinement of models. Featuring full N-Gon support, advanced edge operations, multi-resolution and dynamic subdivision, and Python scripting for custom tools and add-ons, it caters to every modeling requirement with exceptional finesse.

VFX Tracking

Blender, lauded as "probably the best tracker in the market" by VFX professionals, delivers production-ready camera and object tracking with effortless import, masking, and real-time 3D scene viewing. Featuring auto and manual tracking, advanced camera reconstruction, and support for planar tracking and tripod solvers, it's an indispensable tool for VFX.

Animation & Rigging

Blender's powerful rigging and animation tools offer high-quality envelope, skeleton, and automatic skinning, complemented by B-spline interpolated bones, a curve editor, and custom bone shapes for efficient input and sound synchronization.

Story Art

Draw directly in a 3D viewport for enhanced storyboard and 2D artistry, featuring 2D-3D integration, full animation support with onion skinning, customizable layers and colors, and the ability to sculpt brush strokes and parent them to 3D objects.

Tailor Blender to Your Needs

Blender's flexible Python interface allows you to customize layouts, colors, and fonts, support high-resolution screens, create personal tools and add-ons, draw over the OpenGL viewport, and connect with Blender's Render API.
